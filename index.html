04 / About

Why Keel exists

Keel was founded on over a decade spent inside the rooms where civic futures get decided: tripling funding for startup ecosystems, generating millions of dollars in partnerships, advising CEOs and boards, and building the operating systems that turn priorities into results.

The same lesson kept surfacing: extraordinary missions were failing not for lack of purpose or money, but for lack of alignment and execution.

Great coalitions fell apart in the gap between the vision and the Monday-morning meeting. Keel exists to close that gap. We bring the discipline of the best-run companies to the institutions working to build stronger communities, and we stay long enough to see it work.
